# Top 100 Target Companies (Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar & GIFT City)
# 5-Day Work Week & Node.js/Express Backend Focus

This file contains the curated list of 100 technology companies in the Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, and GIFT City region. All listed companies have 100+ employees, officially operate on a strict **5-day work week** (no standard alternate Saturdays or 6-day models), use **Node.js/Express** in their backend engineering teams (either primarily or within dedicated divisions), and meet the minimum compensation baseline of **6+ LPA** for software developers with 2+ years of experience.

Use this file as the primary reference when evaluating job offers, preparing for interviews, or planning applications in the Gujarat region.

---

## 📊 Summary of Tiers

1. **Global Innovators & GIFT City IFSC Pioneers (Ranks 1-17):** Massive MNCs and Global Capability Centers (GCCs) offering global compensation bands (₹12 - ₹35+ LPA) and mature 5-day cultures.
2. **Elite Product & Service Hybrids (Ranks 18-40):** Funded product companies, SaaS platforms, and premium digital engineering firms offering ₹8 - ₹18 LPA. High growth and strong engineering focus.
3. **Standardized Digital Agencies & Staff Augmentation Leaders (Ranks 41-100):** Bedrock of the local ecosystem (100–500+ employees) offering stable 5-day weeks, standard benefits, and ₹6 - ₹10 LPA.
